About Our Company

Our Story

Founded in 2025 as a college project, MyShop has grown from a simple idea to a thriving e-commerce platform. What began as a classroom assignment has transformed into a passion project dedicated to bringing quality products to our customers.

Our Mission

We believe in making online shopping simple, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide high-quality products with exceptional customer service, all while learning and improving our skills in web development.

Why Choose Us?

  • Student-made with attention to detail
  • Simple and user-friendly interface
  • Quality products at competitive prices
  • Continuous improvement based on user feedback
